Hidilyn Diaz Qualifies for Tokyo Olympics

Olympic silver medalist Hidilyn Diaz has qualified for the Tokyo Olympics after her performance in the Asian Weightlifting Championships in Uzbekistan. Diaz lifted a total of 214 kilograms, winning gold in the women's 55-kilogram category.

The Philippine Basketball Association (PBA) season has resumed in a "bubble" format at the Clark Freeport Zone in Pampanga. Strict health protocols are in place to ensure the safety of players and staff.

EJ Obiena Sets New Pole Vault Record

Filipino pole vaulter EJ Obiena set a new national record by clearing 5.87 meters at the World Athletics Indoor Tour in Madrid, Spain. Obiena's previous record was 5.81 meters, which he set in Italy in 2020.
