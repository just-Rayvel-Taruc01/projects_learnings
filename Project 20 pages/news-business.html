Duterte signs law creating Department of Migrant Workers

President Rodrigo Duterte has signed a law creating the Department of Migrant Workers and Overseas Filipinos, which aims to protect the welfare of Filipino migrant workers. The new department will replace the Overseas Workers Welfare Administration and will have more resources and powers to provide better services to Filipino workers abroad.

Philippine economy grows 6.4% in Q4 2022

The Philippine economy grew 6.4% in the fourth quarter of 2022, according to the Philippine Statistics Authority. The growth was attributed to the government's infrastructure projects and the recovery of the tourism industry. The full-year growth for 2022 was 5.9%, surpassing the government's target of 4.5% to 5.5%.

San Miguel Corp. acquires stake in Philippine Airlines

San Miguel Corporation has acquired a 49% stake in Philippine Airlines, the country's flag carrier. The deal is expected to boost the airline's operations and expand its network, as San Miguel has extensive experience in the transportation and logistics industry. The remaining 51% stake will still be held by the Lucio Tan Group.

DOLE orders regularization of 20,000 workers

The Department of Labor and Employment has ordered the regularization of 20,000 workers in various industries, including manufacturing, construction, and retail. The move is in compliance with the government's push to promote labor rights and provide job security for Filipino workers. The affected companies have been given 30 days to comply with the order.
